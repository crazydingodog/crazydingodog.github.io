In memory of Crazy, who could fly

Sweet, loved life, gentle with children, strict with cats. And an ace flyer.

Crazy came from a kind deaf woman who rescued deaf dogs. She matured from a hunky puppy into a lean, determined athlete and loyal companion.

I’m Rick Roberts, and Bonnie and Crazy were my beloved dogs whose long lives mostly overlapped for close to nineteen years.
